Section elements should all have a heading tag in them. In this case, the section title above uses a heading 2 tag. It uses a heading 2 tag because the primary heading of the page uses a heading 1 tag, and headings should be ordered by their level of importance within the document. In case you were wondering, this is a paragraph tag, by the way.

Yet Another Section Title

One thing to note about heading tags: we don't use them because of their style properties. It's nice that headings make text larger and bold, but the main reason we use them is because they communicate structure about the document. An h1 is more important than an h2, which is more important than an h3, and so on until h6, which is the smallest heading tag.
